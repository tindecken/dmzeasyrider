Our Trip

We will pick you up at your hotel around 8:30 am. and then take you on a full day tour to discover Hue.

On the back of a motorbike and with an experienced English-speaking guide, you will be able to stop at many famous sights, please choose from the below:

  1. Tu Hieu pagoda (free entrance)
  2. Vong Canh Hill, Franceand American bunker (free entrance)
  3. Tu Duc Tomb
  4. Minh Mang Tomb
  5. Khai Dinh Tomb
  6. Thien Mu Pagoda (free entrance)
  7. Citadel, including Flag Tower, Ngo Mon Gate, Nine Dynastic Urns, Nine Holy Cannons, Thai Hoa Palace, Forbidden Purple City (Tu Cam Thanh)
  8. Conical Hat and Incense making Village (free entrance)
  9. Thieu Tri tomb (free entrance)
  10. Dong Khanh tomb (free entrance)
  11. Gia Long tomb
  12. Thanh Toan tile roofed Bridge (free entrance)
  • Start time: 8:30 am
  • Finish time: approx 4:30 pm
