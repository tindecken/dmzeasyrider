Hue to Hoi An (or Hoi An to Hue)

Info

Our Trip

We depart from Hue in the morning and ride along the scenic coast route so that you have opportunities to admire the villages, rice paddies, buffalo grazing, …

We'll stop at the fishing village where people still live, mainly on fishing. The second stop is Phuoc Tuong pass. Elephant Springs is our next stop where you can have a bath in a cool stream.

It is named "Elephant Springs" due to it have a rock that looks like an elephant. This is a wonderful natural pool about 30 m large, located between two waterfalls.

Leaving Elephant Springs, we will continue the trip to Hai Van Pass. From the top of this pass, you can see a spectacularnatural scenery: Lang Co Bay - one of the world's most beautiful bays, grand views of the sea, China Beach, Da Nang City,...

Take a rest and then ride down the mountain and go to Da Nang city by road, along the famous China Beach. Before reaching to Hoi An ancient town, we will take you to the last stop, Marble Mountain. Here you can explorethe caves, beautiful views ofour countryand skies, visitthesacredtemple or just admire Non Nuoc rock village.
