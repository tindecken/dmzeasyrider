Our Trip

Day 1: Hue to Prao

With this tour, you come to Hoi An town by taking the Ho Chi Minh trail - a legendary road during the war.

At the first day, our destination is Prao town, Dong Giang district, Quang Nam Province.

Along the Ho Chi Minh trail, you can explore many beautiful landscapes such as the Mau waterfall where you can take a swim in the cool water or meet the Van Kieu ethnic.

We will stop at A Luoi town for the lunch. We continue our trip, discovering the natural landscape and the life of minority ethnic tribes along the border with Laos.

We finish our first day at a hotel in Prao town. You will have your own free time in the evening.

Day 2: Prao town to Hoi An City

Our trip continues, riding along the Ho Chi Minh trail again. Enjoy the fresh air and feel the spirit of freedom travelling on the back of motorbikes with our friendly drivers.

After 52km of this trail, we reach Thanh My bridge and go to My Son sanctuary, a World Heritage site since 1999. We stop there for you to explore the Champatemples with many architectural styles and sculptures representing every historical period of the Kingdom of Champa.

My Sonis considered one of the central temples of Hinduism in Southeast Asia and is the only legacy of this kind in Vietnam.

Arrive to Hoi An town where we finish our two day motorbike tour, crossing the Ho Chi Minh trail. We are sure that you have had an unforgettable trip and discovered parts of the real Vietnam.

You can see more of the real Vietnam with another tour with us. You're welcome!
