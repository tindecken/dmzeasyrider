Tour Hue - DMZ - Quang Tri

Info

Quang Tri is famous for its DMZ tours, because it preserves many remains from the anti-American resistance war, such as the ancient citadel of Quang Tri, Khe Sanh Combat Base, Truong Son National Cemetery, Vinh Moc Tunnels, Hien Luong Bridge and Ben Hai River. Even though the war finished  more than 20 years ago, this DMZ tour will still bring you back to one of the hardest times in Vietnam’s recent history.

Our Trip

We will commence the trip at Hue going to Quang Tri province, on the way we will stop at Long Hung Church and Bo De school.At the Church, you can see lots fire and bullet holes on the wall, evidenceoftheAmerican war.

The next stop is QuangTri Old Citadel, a famous place where fighting took place for 81 days and nights in 1972.

Beside the DMZ sites, we take a rest at Cua Tung beach which is famous for its clean water, silky sands and soft breezes, which make the rows of casuarinas trees whisper. Have a swim and take a lunch with local seafood.

In the afternoon, wego on to Highway No1 and visit Hien Luong bridge which passes over Ben Hai river, which was bombed by the US.

Crossing to the north side of the river, we visit the Vinh Moc tunnels - an underground tunnel system made by the Vinh Moc villagers, after they were bombarded by the US Air force.

Before returning to Hue City, we stop at Truong Son Cemetery to pay our respects to the soldiers who fought and died on the Ho Chi Minh Trail during the American War.
