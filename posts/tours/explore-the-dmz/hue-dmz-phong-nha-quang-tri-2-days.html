Our Trip

Day 1: Overnight in Quang Binh

We pick you up from the hotel around 8:30 am and then depart for Dong Ha town. Arriving at Dong Ha town you will visit the Frame of Church and Bo De school. At the Church, you can see lots fire and bullet holes on its walls, evidenceofthe American war.

The next stop is QuangTri Old Citadel, a famous place, where fighting took place for 81 days and nights in 1972.

We have a short stop at Truong Son cemetery - a memorial to the 10,000 Vietnamese Communist soldiers who sacrificed their lives in the Truong Son mountain during the war.

In the afternoon, we across Hien Luong bridge to visitVinh Moc Tunnels - the bulky and unique system of underground passageways of the Vinh Linh and Quang Tri people.

Then we ride to Quang Binh Province. Check into your hotel. Feel free to relax on the beautiful, white sandy beach of Nhat Le.

Day 2: Visit Phong Nha caves, Khe Sanh combat base. Back Hue

After breakfast, we take the day to visit the Phong Nha caves.

Then we return back to Dong Ha town for lunch.

In the afternoon, we will visit the Dakrong bridge, Ho Chi Minh trail, Paco minority village and Khe Sanh Combat Base. Back to Hue around 5pm. Tour ends.

  • Day 1: Start time: 8:30 am
  • Day 2: Finish time: approx 5:00 pm
