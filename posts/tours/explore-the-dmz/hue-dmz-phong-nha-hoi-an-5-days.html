Our Trip

Day 1: Hue﻿﻿﻿﻿ - DMZ Quang Tri - Phong Nha

From Hue City, we take the Highway No.1 to Quang Tri town where you will explore the DMZ tour.

After passing Dong Ha city we go eastwards to the coast and ride along Cua Viet and Cua Tung beach to Vinh Moc. There we visit the most original underground tunnels, the Ben Hai river and Hien Luong bridge.

We continue to follow highway No.1 again (the same as offered in the 1 day DMZ tour). Now in North Vietnam we pass Dong Hoi city and Hoan Lao town, where we go the west on Ho chi Minh trail to Phong Nha.

Stay overnight in a hotel in Son Trach Town.

Day 2: Phong Nha Cave

In the morning we go by boat to the Phong Nha Cave (wet Cave), 5 km along the Son river and then see the Tien Son cave (dry Cave). Then we lunch near the Caves.

We'll ride on the legendary Ho Chi Minh trail, stop at the botanical garden and visit the Eight Volunteers caves. Enjoy the beautiful scenery, featuring the Gio waterfall or hike on the Eco-trail along the Nuoc Mooc spring. Visit some historical relics and stay overnight again in the hotel.

Day 3: Phong Nha - Khe Sanh

We start in the morning at the Phong Nha Cave and go on the Ho Chi Minh Trail southwards. We ride along the Se Pang Heng River and cross the Sa Mu Pass.

See really beautiful landscapes, that became famous during the American Vietnam War. Further on we pass huge mountains and go through the deep jungle, that let you see part of the original countryside. We reach Khe Sanh town in the evening and stay overnight in a hotel.

Day 4:Khe Sanh - Prao Town

After a night in Khe Sanh we start at 8 am again to go further on highway No. 9, known as the Ho Chi Minh Trail. It is your decision to see Lang Vay closed to the Lao border, before we leave Khe Sanh town.

We come to A Luoi town, pass the famous Dakrong bridge and stop at Ta Rut village to visit the Pa Co ethnic minority.

We continue with the Ho chi minh trail. You will see amazing scenery of primitive forests in the imposing Truong Son mountain range and your photos will be full of many impressive views of wilderness such as: jungles, mountains, waterfalls and the rustic life of the minority people.

We take the lunch at A Luoi town and then ride to Prao town where we will spend a night.

Day 5:Prao town-My Son -Hoi An

Our trip will be continued riding along the Ho Chi Minh trail again. Enjoy the fresh air and feel the spirit of freedom, which riding on the back of motorbikes with our friendly drivers brings.

After 52km of this trail, we reach Thanh My bridge and go to My Son sanctuary, a World Heritage site since 1999. We stop there for you to explore the Champatemples with many architectural styles and sculptures representing every historical period of the Kingdom of Champa.

My Sonis considered one of the central temples of Hinduism in Southeast Asia and is the only legacy of this kind in Vietnam.

We leave My Son sanctuary and continue our motorbike tour to Hoi An Ancient town. Approx 3 pm, we arrive to Hoi An and you will have time to visit the town.
